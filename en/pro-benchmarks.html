Methodology & sources

Where does the data come from?

The top 20 shown is the official ranking HLTV Top 20 Players of 2025 published in January 2026. Stats (HLTV Rating, KAST, ADR, K/D) are 2025 season + Q1 2026 averages as reported by HLTV on player profiles.

Why may rosters and roles differ from the HLTV ranking?

Several important transfers happened after the ranking was published. We verify rosters weekly on Liquipedia to reflect the current competitive reality. Transfers tracked as of April 2026: m0NESY G2 → Falcons, karrigan FaZe → Falcons, ropz MOUZ → Vitality, Twistzz Liquid → FaZe, YEKINDAR Liquid → FURIA.

FV Rating vs HLTV Rating, same thing?

No. The HLTV Rating 2.1 is the official HLTV formula (weighted KPR/DPR/KAST/Impact/ADR). The FV Rating is our in-house index with different weights (4 pillars: Performance / Consistency / Impact / Utility). Both scales are close but not identical. When comparing your FV Rating to pro HLTV Rating, treat it as an "order of magnitude" rather than a strict comparison.

Is the "level" estimate reliable?

The estimate combines your FV Rating and FACEIT ELO to give an order of magnitude of the solo queue → LAN pro gap. Intentionally conservative: a solo queue lvl 9-10 with 1.10 FV Rating is not at the level of a top 100 world LAN pro. Solo queue context (5 randoms vs coached team, mic vs pro tactical comm) creates a gap no stat captures perfectly.

Sync and data freshness

Vercel cron Monday 04:00 UTC: invalidates cache and attempts aggregation from our ingested S-tier matches. If not enough matches in DB, the verified seed remains source of truth. CDN cache s-maxage=900 (15 min) with 1h stale-while-revalidate.

Public API

The endpoint GET /api/pro-benchmarks is open (no auth required). Params: aggregate=overall|map, map=de_inferno, side=ct|t, role=awp|entry|igl|support|rifler. Public JSON response, free to use.
