6. Your Rights

In accordance with articles 15 to 22 of the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of the data concerning you

  • Erasure: request deletion of your account and data

  • Opposition: object to processing for legitimate interest
  • Restriction: request restriction of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent: at any time, without retroactive effect

To exercise these rights, write to contact@fragvalue.com from the email associated with your account. Response within 30 days maximum.

8. Cookies & Audience Measurement

FragValue uses a limited number of cookies, classified into two categories:

Strictly necessary cookies (placed without consent)

  • Authentication (Supabase): session token, persists connected state
  • UI preferences: theme, language, last open tab
  • Cookie consent: remembers your choice (accept / decline) for 30 days to 1 year
  • Anti-fraud (Stripe): payment verification and anti-CSRF

Audience measurement cookies (only after explicit consent)

After clicking "Accept" on the banner on your 1st visit:

  • Google Analytics 4 (_ga, _ga_*): anonymized audience measurement (page views, journey, conversion). Configured with Consent Mode v2 and IP anonymization. Duration: 24 months.

If you click "Decline", GA4 works in signal-only mode without cookie : only aggregated statistics are collected (global page views, no individual identifier). No advertising pixel (Facebook, TikTok, etc.) is ever placed.

You can change your choice at any time by deleting the cookie fv_consent_v1 in your browser settings, the banner will reappear on the next visit.

9. Security

Technical measures in place:

  • Encryption HTTPS/TLS 1.3 on all communications
  • Passwords hashed via bcrypt (never stored in clear text)
  • Signed session tokens (JWT) with expiration
  • Data access protected by Row Level Security (RLS) on Supabase
  • HaveIBeenPwned verification at registration (leaked passwords refused)
  • Regular security audits via Supabase linters and manual testing
