Article 3 - Service description

FragValue is a SaaS web platform for Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) data analysis. The services offered include:

  • Plan Free (gratuit) : analyse de matches FACEIT limitée (3 par mois), statistiques basiques, 1 diagnostic AI Coach par mois.
  • Plan Pro (paye) : analyses unlimited, Advanced KPIs, 2D Replay, Match Report round-by-round, Unlimited AI Coach, Pro demos viewer, comparaison multi-players, export PDF, support prioritaire.
  • Plan Elite (paye, destine aux coachs et equipes) : tout Pro + dashboard equipe, analyse tactique par round, préparation de veto, anti-strat tool, pro benchmarks, pro strats DB et fonctionnalites exclusives pour le coaching.

Detailed features and pricing for each plan are presented on the pricing page. The Vendor reserves the right to modify plan composition while preserving overall value for the Customer.
