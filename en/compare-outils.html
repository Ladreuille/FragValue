Frequently asked questions

I'm already on Leetify, is it worth migrating?
If you play mostly on FACEIT and want precise tactical advice (not just stats), yes. Our Claude AI Coach personalizes your 7-day action plan with pro references and workshop map lineups that Leetify doesn't offer. The more serious you are about progression, the bigger the difference.
Why is FragValue cheaper than Leetify?
We are a 100% bootstrapped FR team, no investors, optimized EU hosting (Vercel + Supabase Frankfurt). Our infrastructure cost is lower and we prefer targeting the French-speaking CS2 community at an accessible price rather than chasing max margin.
Is the AI Coach really useful or a gimmick?
The AI Coach uses Claude Sonnet 4.5 (Pro/Elite) with a prompt enriched with FACEIT tier benchmarks and 2026 pro references. It doesn't tell you "do DM", it tells you "do 400 kills/day on aim_botz USP+AK in ropz style, target 45% HS over 5 matches". Free trial 1x/month on the Free plan, you judge.
If I go Pro then cancel, do I keep my analyses?
Yes. Your past analyses remain accessible from your history even after downgrading to Free. You just won't have access to new Pro features (2D Replay, advanced KPIs, unlimited AI Coach). No data lost.
Can I test without giving my credit card?
You can rester en Free indefiniment avec 3 analyses + 1 AI Coach par mois pour te faire une idee. Si tu veux passer Pro, tu paies des le 1er day mais tu peux annuler en 1 clic et obtenir un remboursement sous 14 days.
Does FragValue work with non-FACEIT demos (Valve matchmaking, ESEA)?
Yes for standard .dem CS2 files (FACEIT, Valve MM, Esportal, ESEA). Our parser handles all official formats. The AI diagnosis is optimized for FACEIT (ELO/level); for other platforms, advice stays tactical but without ELO benchmark.
