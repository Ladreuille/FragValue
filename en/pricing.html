Frequently asked questions

Need clarification?

Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and prorating is calculated automatically.
Y a-t-il un engagement ?
No, all subscriptions are commitment-free. You can cancel anytime from your account.
Le AI Coach, c'est quoi exactement ?
FragValue offre 2 features AI Coach distinctes : (1) le AI diagnosis qui analyse your last 20 FACEIT matches et génère un diagnostic personnalisé (strengths, faiblesses, actions, objectif hebdo, map prioritaire) · refresh after each match en Pro/Elite, sans limite de refresh. (2) le AI Coach Chat (conversational) : you ask questions about your analyzed demos and it answers with clickable links to key rounds · limited to 5 messages/day on Pro, 30/day on Elite, and you can buy additional credit packs (50 for €4 or 200 for €12). Powered by Claude by Anthropic.
Why no free trial? What guarantee do I have?
Tu paies dès le 1er day pour avoir un accès immédiat à toutes les fonctionnalités Pro. You can annuler at any time en 1 clic depuis ton espace compte, sans justification. Et si tu changes d'avis dans les 14 days, on te rembourse intégralement (article L221-18 du Code de la consommation).
How does the 14-day money-back guarantee work?
If within 14 days of purchase you're not happy with Pro, email contact@fragvalue.com and we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. No conditions, no commitment.
Le plan Free est vraiment gratuit ?
Oui, Free est gratuit pour toujours avec 3 analyses de démos par mois, 3 scouts par day, et l'essentiel des stats FACEIT. No credit card required. You can utiliser FragValue sans jamais payer un centime si Free te suffit.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay via our partner Stripe. All payments are secure.
What is the FV Score?
FV Score /100 is our proprietary talent index combining 4 pillars: Performance (35pts), Consistency (28pts), Impact (25pts), and Utility (15pts). More details on our Statistics Guide.
Are demos stored?
Demo files are processed in real-time then deleted from our servers. Only extracted statistics are kept in your account.
Is the Chrome extension required?
The extension is optional but recommended for Pro users. It enables automatic sync of your FACEIT matches without manual uploads.
Who is the Elite plan for?
Elite is designed for coachs, capitaines de team et structures esport qui veulent gérer un roster collectif. Tu obtiens tout Pro + Chat AI Coach 30 messages/day (vs 5 en Pro), dashboard d'équipe, anti-strat tool, préparation veto BO3/BO5 data-driven, et benchmarks vs teams pro HLTV. Si tu joues seul, Pro suffit largement. Pour les questions specifiques aux structures (facturation entreprise, multi-utilisateurs, formation coach), écris à contact@fragvalue.com.
